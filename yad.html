Yadadri temple situated atop the hillock Yadagirigutta, is the pride of young Telangana State. Extensively renovated after the formation of the state, it has become a great tourist attraction besides being a pilgrimage centre. The deity at the temple is Shri Lakshminarasimha Swamy, an incarnation of Lord Vishnu. The temple has large following as was evident from the number of prayer threads tied to the railing around the visitor's pathway.
